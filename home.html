Bio

I am a cofounder and the vice president of corporate development for Everyone Inc.

Immediately before that I cofounded and served as executive director for the Center for New Data, a non-profit technology company dedicated to bringing cutting-edge Silicon Valley data science techniques to academic and public research. Our two initial projects, the COVID Alliance and Observing Democracy, have been featured in the Wall Street Journal, the Washington Post, and Politico.

My previous experience includes in-house corporate strategy, management consulting, investment banking, and tech entrepreneurship.

I currently live in Los Angeles and am both a US and EU citizen. I have worked in North America, Europe, Africa, Asia, and the Middle East. I speak conversational French and Portuguese, as well as basic Spanish and Arabic.
